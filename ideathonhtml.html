Ideathon by IEEE

In the ever-changing world of humanity with technology as our partner, even an atom of thought is to be considered for research and development. The IEEE student branch nit Durgapur organized “Ideathon”, an event to bring about the ideas from the brilliant minds of youngsters of the NIT Durgapur that contains enough prowess to improve the lives of millions and to do better for humanity with the help of technology.
